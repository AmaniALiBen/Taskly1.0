1. Placing an Order

When you place an order on Taskly, you agree to provide clear and complete requirements for your project. Once you submit your requirements and click "Submit & Start Project," your requirements become locked and cannot be changed. This ensures the seller can begin working based on the exact information you provided. You are responsible for reviewing your requirements before submission.

2. Payment Terms

Full payment is required before any seller begins working on your order. Your payment is held securely in Taskly's escrow system. Funds are only released to the seller after you review and accept the delivered work. You will not be charged any additional platform fees beyond the displayed price. The service fee is included in the total amount shown at checkout.

3. Order Completion

You have 7 days to review the delivered work after a seller submits it. During this review period, you can either accept the delivery (which releases payment to the seller and marks the order as completed) or request a revision (asking the seller to make changes to the work). If you do not take any action within 7 days, the order may be automatically marked as completed.

4. Revisions

Each order comes with 3 free revisions (unless the gig specifies otherwise). Revision requests must be clear and specific about what changes you need. Additional revisions beyond the free limit may require extra payment. Revision requests cannot be made after you have accepted the delivery.

5. Cancellation Policy

You may request to cancel an order under the following conditions: Before submitting requirements - full refund. After requirements are locked but no work delivered - 50% refund. After delivery has been submitted - no refund unless approved through dispute resolution. All cancellation requests are reviewed by our support team. Refunds are returned to your Taskly wallet balance.

6. Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with the delivered work and cannot reach an agreement with the seller, you can file a complaint using the "Report Issue" button on the order page. Our support team will review your complaint within 24 hours and may request additional evidence. Possible resolutions include requesting additional revisions, partial refund, full refund, or order cancellation. All dispute decisions are final and binding.

You agree to communicate respectfully with sellers at all times. Harassment, abusive language, or any form of disrespectful behavior may result in account suspension. All communication regarding an order should take place within the Taskly messaging system. Sharing personal contact information before order completion is prohibited.

As a buyer on Taskly, you agree to: provide clear and detailed project requirements, review deliveries within a reasonable timeframe, respond to seller messages in a timely manner, release payment when work is satisfactory, request revisions respectfully within the agreed limits, and not request work outside the original scope without additional payment.

You are responsible for keeping your PIN confidential. Taskly will never ask for your PIN outside of the payment verification process.
